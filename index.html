Welcome to the sector! You've recently acquired an old refueling station and installed a small shop. You are currently alone, but you should hire staff, buy your own ships, and manage the station wisely so it grows over time. Can you reach ᖬ10 Million?

Goods and fuel purchases happen automatically while you have visitors or residents. Merchants will restock your stores, but you must order fuel yourself. More visitors and residents will bring more frequent transactions. The various staff positions have different effects on the economy and station operations.

All staff members cost ᖬ500 a week. If a department is lacking, you will be informed. Staff can be laid off by opening the dropdown and pressing the 'delete' key while hovering over that position.

Once the station becomes popular, you will need to address crime, maintenenace and medical issues. It's okay to be proactive, but don't hire staff you can't afford!

If you are more ᖬ20k in debt on payday, you lose. Earn a million credits to win. Good luck!
